About University of the East Ramon Magsaysay Memorial Medical Center (UERMMMC)

Founded in 1956, UERMMMC in Quezon City, Philippines, specializes in health sciences. It is named in honor of former Philippine President Ramon Magsaysay and is known for its focus on healthcare education and research.

  • Rules: UERMMMC enforces a dress code that requires professional attire for clinical settings, white uniforms for nursing, and lab coats for laboratory-based courses.
