About St. Paul University Quezon City (SPUQC)

Founded in 1946, SPUQC is a Catholic institution run by the Sisters of St. Paul of Chartres. It emphasizes holistic education rooted in faith, excellence, and service.

  • Rules: SPUQC has a formal dress code policy, mandating uniforms for various courses and events. Proper attire is also expected for church-related activities.
