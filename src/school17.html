Pasay City Science National High School

Pasay City National Science High School (PCNSHS) is a public secondary educational institution in Pasay City, Philippines. Established to foster academic excellence, the school specializes in science, mathematics, and technology education. As part of the national network of Science High Schools, PCNSHS aims to develop competent and responsible individuals prepared for higher education and significant contributions to society. The school provides a conducive learning environment focused on innovation and scientific inquiry.

  • Vision - Mission:Pasay City National Science High School is a center of excellence in science education, cultivating young learners to become globally competitive, socially responsible, and morally upright individuals. We aim to shape future leaders with a strong foundation in scientific and technological expertise, coupled with unwavering dedication to ethical principles and community service.
  • Our Core Values: Academic Excellence and Innovation – Integrity and Respect – Responsibility and Discipline

Available Strand

  • STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics) Strand
