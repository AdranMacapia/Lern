About AMA Computer University

Founded in 1980, AMA is the first IT-focused university in the Philippines and has since expanded its programs in various fields. It has several campuses, with its main campus in Quezon City.

  • Rules: AMA students follow a dress code, emphasizing business or professional attire for certain academic courses. Students in tech-related fields may wear specified uniforms.

  • Bachelor of Science in Electronics and Communications Engineering
  • Bachelor of Science in Business Administration
  • Bachelor of Science in Accountancy
  • Bachelor of Science in Psychology
