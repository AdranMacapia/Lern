Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I search for schools on School Gallery?
    You can search for schools by using the search function on our homepage. Simply select a location and/or course type, and click the search button to view relevant results.
  • Are the school listings on School Gallery up-to-date?
    We strive to keep all our listings as current as possible. However, we recommend contacting the schools directly to confirm the most recent information.
  • How can I add my school to School Gallery?
    If you'd like to add your school to our gallery, please contact us through our Contact page. We'll review your request and get back to you with further instructions.
  • Is School Gallery free to use?
    Yes, School Gallery is completely free for students and parents to use. We do not charge any fees for searching or viewing school information.
  • Can I leave reviews for schools on School Gallery?
    Currently, we do not have a review system in place. However, we're working on implementing this feature in the near future to provide more comprehensive information to our users.
