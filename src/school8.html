About Taguig Science High School

Taguig Science High School (TSHS) (Filipino: Mataas ng Paaralang Pang-Agham ng Tagig), also known as TagSci or TagSat, is a public science high school located in San Miguel, Taguig, Philippines. It is one of the three science high schools of the city, and is managed and administered by the Schools Divisions Office of Taguig City and Pateros (SDO-TaPat). TSHS traces its roots from the Special Science class of Signal Village National High School.   • The mission of Taguig Science High School (TSHS) is to create a safe and supportive environment for children, where their voices are heard, their dreams are nurtured, and their lives are cherished
  • Science, Math, and English curriculum: TSHS specializes in these subjects
  • Senior High School (SHS): TSHS offers two tracks in SHS:
    • Science and Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM)
    • Accountancy and Business Management (ABM)
