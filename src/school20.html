San Isidro National High School

San Isidro National High School (SINHS) is a public secondary educational institution located in Makati City, Philippines. The school is committed to providing quality education that fosters academic excellence, moral integrity, and social responsibility among its students. SINHS offers a comprehensive curriculum designed to equip learners with the necessary skills and knowledge to succeed in higher education and future careers.

  • Vision - Mission:San Isidro National High School is a nurturing, responsive, and innovative public institution for the youth. We cultivate an educative community of lifelong learners, advocates of environmental sustainability, and agents of social transformation. We deliver holistic education that empowers the youth, especially the underprivileged, to become responsible and upright citizens.
