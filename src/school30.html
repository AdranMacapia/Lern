About Trinity University of Asia (TUA)

Established in 1963 in Quezon City, TUA is a Christian university affiliated with the Episcopal Church. Known for its commitment to social responsibility, it provides a range of academic programs and emphasizes a values-based education.

  • Rules: TUA requires students to adhere to a semi-formal dress code, particularly on campus grounds. Specific attire guidelines apply to different colleges and activities.

  • Bachelor of Science in Tourism Management
