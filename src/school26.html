About Far Eastern University Diliman

Far Eastern University (FEU) Diliman was established in 1994 as a branch of the main Far Eastern University in Manila. It offers programs in business, engineering, and other disciplines. The Diliman campus serves as an extension of FEU's commitment to providing quality education in the Philippines. The university aims to provide students with academic excellence and holistic development, offering a modern campus with updated facilities to support various learning opportunities.

  • Rules: Far Eastern University Diliman requires students to adhere to a dress code, wearing uniforms during designated academic and laboratory sessions. The university upholds regulations on student conduct, stressing the importance of respect for faculty, staff, and fellow students. Punctuality for classes is mandatory, and students must maintain a minimum grade point average (GPA) to remain in good academic standing. Students are expected to contribute to maintaining a clean and orderly campus and avoid engaging in any disruptive behavior. Additionally, ethical conduct is expected both within and outside the university community.
  • College of Business Administration: BS in Business Administration, Accounting, Management Accounting
  • College of Engineering: BS in Civil, Computer, ELectronics, Electrical, and Industrial Engineering
  • College of Arts and Sciences: BA in Communication, and Psychology, BS in Information System, and Computer Science
  • College of Education: Bachelor of Elementary Education, and Secondary Education
  • Graduate Programs: Masters of Business Administration, Masters of Science in Engineering Management
