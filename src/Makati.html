Makati City

Makati City is home to a diverse range of top educational institutions, including international schools, business schools, and specialized institutions like Ateneo Graduate School of Business and Makati Science High School. The city's institutions are recognized for their strong academic programs, particularly in business, technical-vocational skills, and science.
