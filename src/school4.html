About British School Manila

The British School Manila was established in 1976 in the old Union Church with only 36 students. Over the course of almost fifty years, the school has grown, developed and evolved into what it is today. Designed to offer the students a wider sense of the visual and performing arts.

  • Core Values: Respect, Integrity and Kindness are woven into the fabric of our school community and shared principles.
