About Don Bosco Technical Institute Makati

Don Bosco Technical Institute Makati (DBTI) is a Catholic educational institution established in 1963 by the Salesians of Don Bosco. The school is part of the global network of Don Bosco schools, which focus on providing quality education rooted in the Salesian values of reason, religion, and loving-kindness. DBTI aims to form young individuals into competent and responsible citizens, with an emphasis on technical and vocational training, academic excellence, and holistic development.

  • Vision - Mission:Don Bosco – Makati is a nurturing, responsive, research-oriented, and innovative Salesian school for the young. We form an educative-pastoral community of lifelong-learners, champions of integral ecology, and agents of social transformation. We provide a holistic Catholic education that forms the young, especially the poor, to become good Christians and upright citizens.
  • Our Core Values: Spirituality – Collaboration – Integrity
