Philippine State College of Aeronautics

The Philippine State College of Aeronautics (PhilSCA) is a premier state institution dedicated to providing advanced education and training in aviation and aerospace sciences. With multiple campuses across the Philippines, including the main campus in Villamor, Pasay City, PhilSCA offers a range of undergraduate and graduate programs aimed at producing highly skilled professionals in the aviation industry. The college emphasizes a blend of theoretical knowledge and practical experience, ensuring that graduates are well-prepared to meet the demands of the global aviation sector.

  • Vision - Mission:The Philippine State College of Aeronautics (PhilSCA) envisions itself as a leading institution in aviation and aerospace education, committed to excellence in instruction, research, and community service. We aim to produce globally competitive professionals who contribute to national development and the advancement of the aviation industry. We foster a culture of innovation, ethical standards, and social responsibility to prepare students for the dynamic demands of the global
  • Our Core Values: Excellence – Integrity – Innovation - Social Responsibility
