About Assumption College

Assumption College (AC), founded in 1951 by the Religious of the Assumption (RA), is a Catholic institution located in San Lorenzo Village, Makati. The college is known for its strong academic programs and commitment to the holistic development of young women. AC integrates Christian values, intellectual rigor, and personal growth to shape future leaders who are academically excellent, socially responsible, and spiritually grounded. The school offers programs from Preschool to Senior High School and emphasizes a culture of empowerment, discipline, and compassion.

  • Mission:Inspired by St. Marie Eugenie, we passionately form persons and communities of faith and action through a transformative education that nurtures faith and spirituality, pursues academic excellence for service and instills social responsibility, thus “forging character for life and life for God.” (SME)
  • Vision: An innovative Catholic college committed to excellent education forming women servant leaders for social transformation.
