St. Paul College of Pasay

St. Paul College of Pasay (SPCP) is a private Catholic educational institution located in Pasay City, Philippines. Founded by the Sisters of St. Paul of Chartres, SPCP is part of a global network of Paulinian institutions dedicated to providing quality education rooted in Catholic values. The school offers programs from basic education to tertiary levels, aiming to develop students into well-rounded individuals committed to academic excellence, moral integrity, and social responsibility.

  • Vision - Mission:St. Paul College of Pasay is a nurturing, responsive, and innovative Catholic institution for the young. We form an educative-pastoral community of lifelong learners, champions of integral ecology, and agents of social transformation. We provide holistic Catholic education that forms the young, especially the underprivileged, to become good Christians and upright citizens.
  • Our Core Values: Excellence – Integrity – Innovation - Social Responsibility
